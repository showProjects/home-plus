Are We Losing Our Identity?

Accountant-designed rather than architect-designed. Profit-maximizing rather than prosperity-maximizing. Factory farmed rather than free range. Singapore has become a country swarmed by skyscrapers. Some of us might be grasping for a more permanent reminder of who we are. For building blocks for our identities, you could say.

Kampong Spirit

Enter Figment. Grounded in the gritty yet sexy red light district of Geylang. Where we first embarked on boutique development The Lorong 24A Shophouse Series.

Oh, kampong means ‘village’ in Malay. We’d love to restore a sense of community and belonging, or kampong spirit, to modern urban living.

Figment Today

We have curated a collection of more than 30 unique shophouses. More than a thousand members have joined us on our mission to place make our local neighbourhoods.

65 local artists. 17 design studios. 5 tour agencies. 3 startup incubators. And countless proud Singaporeans and expats alike.

Welcome To The Neighbourhood

Placemaking the ’15-min Kampong’. Imagine from the time you wake, everything you need is right there within a ‘15-min’ walk. The local bakery down the block. A co-working space two blocks away. And a yoga studio to wind down after work opposite where you live. Say hello to ‘free-range’ living.

What’s not to like about vibrant, mixed-use living? Condo-me-not!
